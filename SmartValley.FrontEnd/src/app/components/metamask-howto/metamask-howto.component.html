Our Service using Ethereum blockchain
If you want to participate you need to download Metamask

Metamask is an Ethereum Browser, like Mist! It allows you all the same functions, features and ease of access from reqular Ethereum Wallets, and it allows you to interact with Dapps and Smart Contracts, and all without the need to download the blockchain or install any software, you can just install it as a Google Chrome Extension or a Firefox Add-on!

Part 1 - Installing Metamask

Step 1

Click here to go to the chrome WebStore and get Metamask Plugin

Step 2

Click "Add to Chrome" to install Metamask as Google chrome extensions

Step 3

Click "Add Extension" to confirm and Metamask will be added

Part 1 - Installing Metamask

Step 1

Click here to go to the Firefox Add-ons and get Metamask Plugin

Step 2

Click "Add to Firefox" to install Metamask

Step 3

Click "Add" to confirm and Metamask will be added

Part 2 - Creating a new wallet

Step 1

Click the Metamask logo

Step 2

Read and accept to the Metamask terms and Conditions

Step 3

Click "Main Network"

Step 4

Change Main Network to "Rinkeby Test Network"

Step 5

Enter a strong password and click "Create"

Step 6

Metamask will now show you your seed. It is very important that you copy and store those 12 words, without them you cannot restore your wallet. Click "I've copied it somewhere safe"

Congratulations! Your account is now created!
